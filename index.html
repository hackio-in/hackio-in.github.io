What we do

Learn. Teach. Build.

School students have a lot of ideas to solve problems around them and build the next big thing but are unable to work on them due to a lack of relevant skills. Schools themselves have no incentive to solve this problem. We want to fill this gap by equipping such students with the skills they need to take their ideas to fruition.

Society in general also does not empathize with the dreams of young creators and this can definitely change if they get more support. We will approach schools to let some of their interested students be a part of our 5-day induction program that will teach them skills like python programming, machine learning, and sensor networking. After this, these students will go on to be a part of our integrated community of problem solvers. We will leverage the collective potential of the community by helping people collaborate on project ideas.

The Hackio Bootcamp

We, the students who started the Kumaran Hacker Maker Designer Community have now started a venture named Hackio. We are conducting a 5-day guided hackathon for school students where they will be introduced to the basics of programming in a fun and simple way and then taken all the way to a level where they can actually make something useful.
